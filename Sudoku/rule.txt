                                 ========{--SUDOKU--}========

   (*) You need to understand the rules of Sudoku to be able to become an "expert" of this game.

(1) Please fill in the necessary information required when participating in the game and you can continue to participate with that account next time if you want.

(2) Sudoku is an intellectual game based on a 9×9 grid divided into small cells.

(3) The numbers to be filled in are positive integers with 1 digit and greater than 0 (from 1 to 9).

(4) Each small 3×3 cube can only contain each number from 1 to 9 once.

(5) Each vertical column can only contain each number from 1 to 9 once.

(6) Each horizontal row can only contain each number from 1 to 9 once.

(7) No numbers in the same 3×3 block, vertical column or horizontal row are allowed to be the same.

(8) The game is considered completed when all numbers are filled in the Sudoku grid without violating the above rules.

(9) You have five chances for each wrong choice, before being informed that you have lost.

(10) When you win, your score will be recorded and on the achievement rankings.
